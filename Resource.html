Recommended Textbooks

  • Mechanical Engineering
    • "Engineering Mechanics: Dynamics" by J.L. Meriam and L.G. Kraige
    • "Thermodynamics: An Engineering Approach" by Yunus A. Çengel and Michael A. Boles
    • "Fluid Mechanics" by Frank M. White
    • "Heat and Mass Transfer" by Yunus A. Çengel
    • "Mechanics of Materials" by R.C. Hibbeler
  • Electrical Engineering
    • "Electric Circuits" by James W. Nilsson and Susan Riedel
    • "Fundamentals of Electric Circuits" by Charles K. Alexander and Matthew N.O. Sadiku
    • "Control Systems Engineering" by Norman S. Nise
    • "Power System Analysis and Design" by J. Duncan Glover and Mulukutla S. Sarma
    • "Digital Signal Processing" by John G. Proakis and Dimitris K. Manolakis
  • Civil Engineering
    • "Structural Analysis" by R.C. Hibbeler
    • "Principles of Geotechnical Engineering" by Braja M. Das
    • "Traffic and Highway Engineering" by Nicholas J. Garber and Lester A. Hoel
    • "Hydrology and Hydraulic Systems" by Ram S. Gupta
    • "Construction Project Management" by Frederick Gould and Nancy Joyce
  • Computer Engineering
    • "Introduction to Algorithms" by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein
    • "Operating System Concepts" by Abraham Silberschatz, Peter B. Galvin, and Greg Gagne
    • "Computer Networks" by Andrew S. Tanenbaum and David J. Wetherall
    • "Software Engineering: A Practitioner's Approach" by Roger S. Pressman
    • "Embedded Systems: Introduction to the MSP432 Microcontroller" by Jonathan W. Valvano

Software and Tools

  • MATLAB - A high-level language and interactive environment for numerical computation, visualization, and programming.
  • AutoCAD - A commercial computer-aided design (CAD) and drafting software application.
  • SolidWorks - A solid modeling computer-aided design and computer-aided engineering computer program.
  • Python - A programming language that lets you work quickly and integrate systems more effectively.
  • ANSYS - A comprehensive software suite for engineering simulation and 3D design.
  • Eclipse - An integrated development environment (IDE) used in computer programming.
